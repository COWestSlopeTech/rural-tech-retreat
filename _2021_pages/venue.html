September 10 - 12
At Chair Mountain Ranch

Chair Mountain Ranch is a seven acre chunk of land with five cabins, two apartments, small pond, and an outdoor common area (gazebo) with BBQs.

178 COUNTY ROAD 3, CARBONDALE, CO 81623

Lodging

All tickets can be purchased on the Eventbrite page here.

Cabin

There are five cabins each with 2 bedrooms, a small kitchen, and small living space.

Apartments

There are two apartments, both in the same red building (see photo). Apartments each have two bedrooms with large kitchens and living space. These are the most comfortable options.

Van / Small RV

Van / small RV spots are in a gravel parking area next to the apartments and along the Ranch driveway.

Camping

Camping spots will be in the large grass field at the center of the Ranch.
